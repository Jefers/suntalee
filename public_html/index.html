Location

Suntalee House is a family run holiday accommodation hotel business. We are situated in a quiet neighbourhood about 3km from Phuket airport and 800m from the beautiful tranquillity of Nai Yang beach and Sirinat National Park. There are many restaurants and bars to choose from in the vicinity. Close to markets and convenience stores.
